INSTRUCTIONS:
1. Click on the 'Instructions' button to open the instructions of usage.
2. Enter relevant details(i.e. number of days and name of subjects) in the appropriate input fields.
3. Click on the 'Generate Timetable' button in order to open the created timetable in a pop-up box.
4. Click on the 'Download Timetable to Desktop' button in order to save the created timetable onto your Desktop.
5. Click on the 'QUIT' button to close the application.
